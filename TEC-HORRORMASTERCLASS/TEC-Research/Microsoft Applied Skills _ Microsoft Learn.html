Who should earn an Applied Skills credential?

  • IT professionals and developers

    Stay ahead in your field. Solve real cloud and AI challenges in a lab environment and prove you’re ready for the projects that matter. These credentials show employers you can hit the ground running on critical tasks.

  • Students and educators

    Bridge the gap between learning and doing. Students can apply what they’ve learned in class to real-world scenarios and prove they’re ready for internships or jobs in tech. Educators can use these credentials to better prepare students for real-world projects.

  • Career changers and career builders

    Launch or grow your career in IT with confidence. These credentials help you gain practical, job-relevant skills for your new path. Show what you can do—and step into your next role ready to succeed.
